How to build a powerful AI VR Virtual Assistant with AWS AL/ML service and OpenAI?

event 2023-06-10 (Day 2) ~ 16:10 - 17:20
explore Function Hall
  • comment Cantonese
  • network_check Intermediate

AWS AI Assistant is a serverless applications and integrates most of the AWS AI/ML services and Open AI.
The most popular tech news in 2023 is the raise of generative AI with Large language models. With those powerful pre-built model or AI API, we can build a very powerful virtual assistant with the combination and integration of all AWS AI/ML services with OpenAI API or Hugging Face pre-trained model. For my use case, I hope my students learn and use AI but I do want to have certain control and monitoring. So, we want to make it fun and powerful for my students.

Cyrus Wong

IVE / Hong Kong

Cyrus Wong is a Senior Lecturer at the Department of Information Technology (IT) at the Hong Kong Institute of Vocational Education (Lee Wai Lee) and an AWS Machine Learning Hero. He’s a passionate advocate for cloud technology and actively promotes its use through various media and events. His projects have won four Hong Kong ICT Awards in 2014, 2015, 2016, and 2022. All of his winning projects run solely on AWS using Data Science and Machine Learning services.

Cyrus Wong is an AWS Academy Accredited Trainer who delivers the full-time course ‘Higher Diploma in Cloud and Data Centre Administration,’ the first tertiary course in Asia dedicated to providing Cloud Technology training. He’s passionate about incorporating AI/ML into his daily curriculum and often writes about projects with his students. In this talk, he’ll share his development team’s journey with you.
